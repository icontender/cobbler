Additional Information

The Cobbler Wiki is a place for reading and submitting documentation, how-to articles, and other content. Any one can add content and is encouraged to do so. For information on creating an account see the Fedora Account Systems page. koan also has some Wiki space, though in general, content should be posted to the Cobbler Wiki with the exception of koan bug reports and features.

More Advanced Topics

Information on the cobbler API, the Triggers mechanism (for interfacing with other programs), and Cobbler's XMLRPC interface are kept on the Developer Docs section of the Wiki. For questions on this content, stop by the IRC channel or mailing list.
